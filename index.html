CORE PRINCIPLES
🤝
AI Assists, Humans Decide
Always keep humans at the center for final strategic decisions. AI is a force-multiplier, not the decision-maker.
🚢
"Ship Small, Ship Often"
Use Feature Flags to release incrementally (5% → 100%) to minimize risk and enable fast rollback.
🧰
Avoid Tool Sprawl
Limit to max 2 AI tools per phase to avoid waste and keep the workflow focused and efficient.
